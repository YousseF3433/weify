Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services does Webify offer?

    Webify specializes in custom web development, e-commerce solutions, WordPress, and company portfolio websites.

  • Do you offer mobile-friendly designs and good for SEO?

    Yes, we offer fully responsive designs compatible with all devices, with a focus on optimizing websites for search engines (SEO) to ensure outstanding performance in search results.

  • How long does it take to build a website?

    The timeline for your project depends on its complexity. A standard WordPress website typically takes 2 to 15 days to complete, while a custom-built site using native code can take between 3 to 25 days. We'll provide you with an accurate timeline based on your specific project requirements.

  • Do you offer training for managing e-commerce stores after delivery?

    Yes, we provide customized training videos for e-commerce stores to help you manage your online store easily. These videos cover essential tasks such as updating products, managing inventory, and processing orders. Additionally, we offer technical support to assist you with any challenges or questions you may encounter while managing your store.

  • Is it possible to upgrade my website later?

    Yes, while our websites are built with scalability in mind, it's important to note that expanding features on a WordPress site can become challenging as your business grows. For long-term growth and flexibility, we recommend considering a custom-built native site, which allows for smoother scalability and more tailored features.
